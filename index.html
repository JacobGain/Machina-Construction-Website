Our Mission & Vision

At Machina Construction Ltd. we are dedicated to delivering high-quality, innovative infrastructure solutions within the sewer and watermain industry while protecting the environment and upholding the highest standards of safety and community responsibility. Guided by integrity and a commitment to excellence, we continuously advance through education, technology, and the development of a skilled, diverse workforce. Our vision is to be a leading force in modern infrastructure, shaping the future of the industry through innovation, sustainability, and a steadfast commitment to empowering the next generation of professionals.

Our Services

Large and Small Diameter Sewer and Watermain Installation

Deep utility installation delivered with precise grade control.

We provide services for both municipal and private-sector projects, ranging from small-scale to large-scale open-cut construction. Our team has extensive experience working in a wide variety of ground conditions and manages intricate, complex underground infrastructure. Committed to excellence, we have successfully completed numerous challenging projects with precision, efficiency, and reliability.

Stormwater Management

Drainage systems built to control flow, erosion, and runoff.

At Machina, we have extensive experience in the installation of storm systems and drainage infrastructure designed to effectively manage stormwater runoff while consistently meeting project specifications and environmental standards.

Earthworks

Bulk excavation and grading shaped for efficient site readiness.

Our earthworks scopes cover excavation, backfill, rough grading, and material movement with an emphasis on production efficiency, subgrade preparation, and maintaining workable conditions across the site.
