Our Core Services

  • Large-Diameter Sewer Main Installation

    Our team specializes in the precise installation of large-diameter sewer mains. Utilizing both traditional open-cut methods and advanced trenchless technology, we deliver efficient, compliant solutions that minimize community disruption. From initial planning and regulatory permitting through to final testing and commissioning, we ensure every pipeline meets the highest standards for durability, performance, and environmental protection.

  • Watermain Installation & Rehabilitation

    Machina Construction provides end-to-end watermain services for both new developments and system upgrades. We collaborate with municipal engineers and developers to design networks that guarantee reliable water delivery. Our process includes hydrostatic testing, chlorination, and commissioning, ensuring long-term integrity and safety for critical water distribution infrastructure.

  • Stormwater Management Systems

    Effective stormwater management is essential to protect communities and natural habitats from flooding and erosion. We design and construct storm sewer networks, retention and detention basins, and engineered drainage channels that comply with environmental regulations. Our crews employ best practices in erosion control and sediment management to safeguard waterways and reduce flood risk on every project.

  • Complete Site Servicing Solutions

    As a trusted site servicing contractor, Machina Construction delivers turn-key solutions for subdivisions, commercial developments, and capital works. We coordinate excavation, grading, and installation of all utilities—including sewer, water, stormwater, and electrical—to ensure seamless integration with existing networks. Our experienced crews manage every aspect of site preparation, enabling developers to advance quickly and efficiently.
