Our Services

Large Diameter Sewer Main Installation

Deep utility installation delivered with precise grade control.

Our team installs large-diameter sewer infrastructure for municipal, subdivision, and capital works projects using disciplined excavation, shoring, bedding, and testing practices that keep production moving while protecting adjacent services and public space.

Large and Small Diameter Watermain Installation

Reliable water infrastructure for new builds and system upgrades.

We deliver full watermain installation scopes, from excavation and pipe placement through pressure testing, chlorination, and commissioning, with the attention to detail needed for both small-diameter runs and major transmission work.

Stormwater Management

Drainage systems built to control flow, erosion, and runoff.

Machina constructs storm sewer networks, chambers, outfalls, and related drainage infrastructure that help developments manage runoff responsibly and meet project drainage and environmental requirements.

Site Servicing

Coordinated underground utility work that keeps sites moving.

We provide integrated site servicing for subdivisions, commercial sites, and municipal projects by coordinating excavation, utility installation, grading, and restoration so each phase connects cleanly with the next.

Pre Construction Services

Early planning that de-risks schedule, scope, and site logistics.

Before crews mobilize, we support projects with constructability input, sequencing, utility planning, procurement coordination, and site approach reviews that help owners and developers make cleaner decisions up front.

Earthworks

Bulk excavation and grading shaped for efficient site readiness.

Our earthworks scopes cover excavation, backfill, rough grading, and material movement with an emphasis on production efficiency, subgrade preparation, and maintaining workable conditions across the site.
