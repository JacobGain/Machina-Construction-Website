Key Policies

Health & Safety Policy Statement

We provide and maintain a safe and healthy work environment to prevent injuries and illnesses. Supervisors ensure safe work practices; workers are trained and competent. We meet or exceed legislative requirements and review objectives annually for continual improvement.

Young & New Workers

Enhanced orientation, mentorship, and close supervision. Required training: Company Orientation, WHMIS, Worker Safety Awareness. New workers are paired with a mentor until competency is verified.

Violence, Harassment & Conflict Management

Zero tolerance policy with clear reporting, investigation steps, and training in respectful communication and de-escalation. Annual risk assessments and controls are documented.
